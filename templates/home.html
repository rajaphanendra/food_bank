Welcome to Food Bank

Currently, we are observing an alarming increase in the number of individuals experiencing severe hunger, with some unfortunate cases resulting in loss of life. Considering this distressing situation, a small group of volunteers has taken the initiative to organize food donations. Generous donors can contribute various packaged food items such as bread, fruits, cookies, and more to the local food bank, thereby providing essential nourishment to those in need.
